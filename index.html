About Me

Having been born in the Mid West, raised in the South East, and now residing in the Pacific North West, (with many adventures in between), Eric consider's himself fairly well traveled. These various settings have allowed him to have plethora of experience with regards to different beliefs and view points, ideas and ambitions, and so on. A man of 28 years on this Earth, he has had several academic and professional journeys that are well on the way of guiding his existing path.
